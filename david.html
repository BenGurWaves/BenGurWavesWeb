David Ivanyuk

David Ivanyuk is the reason BenGurWaves has a face as well as a sound. From the very beginning, he challenged me to think beyond music and to see every track as part of a larger story. He pushed me out of the comfort zone of just making songs, and into building a world where visuals and sound carry equal weight.

Every video, every piece of artwork, every image tied to BenGurWaves comes through David’s lens. His direction gives shape to the atmosphere you feel before the first note even plays.

He doesn’t just create visuals — he translates the music into cinematic form, making sure the project speaks both to the ear and the eye.
